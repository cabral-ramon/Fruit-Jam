Fruit Jam

Fruit-Jam is a match-3 game similar to bejeweled.


First click on a fruit and then click on any other fruit adjacent to the fruit to swap them.


Match three of the same fruits either vertically or horizontally. Your goal is to find as many matches as you can in the given time.


Good Luck!
